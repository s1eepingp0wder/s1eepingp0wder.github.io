Favourite Blur Songs

Beetlebum

I love this song because the riffs are amazing. Damon does a great job explaining the effect of addiction on oneself and others. I relate closely to this song. I use it to cope with my own issues.

Boys and Girls

I love Boys and Girls because it explores bisexuality. I wasn't sure on my sexuality for the longest time but then I learned I was bi. I felt pretty bad at first, like I was greedy or looking for attention. Boys and Girls is a positive song, and it promotes self-love.

Coffee and TV

This song gives me recovery vibes. It reminds me of bouncing back from something traumatic. Starting new. Healing and stuff. It reminds me of becoming a better person and emerging even stronger than before.
